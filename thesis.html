Quantum Cellular Automata and toy models for theoretical physics

Subject:
In our current work, under the name Quantum Cellular Automata and toy models for theoretical physics, we are interested in the development of the strengths and limitations of quantum cellular automata like models to describe physical laws and their symmetries. Thus we are lead to some intersections of different areas such as quantum information, mathematical physics and quantum mechanics of open quantum systems.
